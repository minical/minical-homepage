Frequently asked questions.

Everything you need to know about running an AI-first, white-label hotel PMS on MiniCal.

What is MiniCal? +
MiniCal is a white-label, AI-powered property management system (PMS) for property managers, hotel chains, and franchises. It gives you a complete platform to manage bookings, rates, and operations across one property or a multi-brand portfolio — launched under your own brand.
What is a white-label PMS? +
A white-label PMS is property management software you run under your own brand rather than the vendor's. MiniCal is 100% white-label from the ground up: your logo, your domain, and your brand — MiniCal stays invisible to your customers.
Can I run MiniCal under my own brand? +
Yes. MiniCal is fully white-label. You launch the platform under your own brand, logo, and domain, and can even build and operate your own PMS company on top of it.
How many properties can I manage with MiniCal? +
From one boutique property to a thousand-key franchise, all on the same platform. MiniCal is multi-property by design, with granular roles and per-property settings so you can manage an entire portfolio from one account.
Who is MiniCal for? +
MiniCal is built for PMS companies, hotel chains, franchises, and property managers who want a flexible, brandable platform that scales from a single calendar to a multi-brand portfolio.
What integrations and extensions does MiniCal offer? +
MiniCal ships with everything a property runs on, then extends with 70+ modular extensions for payments, channel managers, OTAs, booking engines, reporting, fiscal compliance, POS, and more. You switch on only what you need as you grow. See the full features & extensions.
How does MiniCal use AI? +
AI is built into every workflow. MiniCal offers dynamic pricing, predictive analytics, demand forecasting, an AI concierge for guest communication, smart revenue management, and automated operations — all designed to maximize revenue and reduce manual work.
